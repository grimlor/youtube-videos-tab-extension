# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2, 2026**

## Overview

YouTube Videos Tab is a browser extension that respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data.

## Data Collection

**YouTube Videos Tab collects ZERO data.**

Specifically:
- ❌ No browsing history
- ❌ No personal information
- ❌ No cookies or local storage
- ❌ No analytics or telemetry
- ❌ No tracking pixels
- ❌ No user identifiers
- ❌ No network requests to external servers

## How the Extension Works

The extension operates entirely locally in your browser:

1. **Detection**: Checks if the current page is a YouTube channel URL
2. **Navigation**: If on a channel main page, navigates to the Videos tab
3. **That's it**: No data leaves your browser

## Permissions

The extension requires minimal permissions:

- **Content Scripts on YouTube.com**: Required to detect channel pages and navigate to the Videos tab
- **No additional permissions**: The extension does not request access to:
  - Your browsing history
  - Your personal data
  - Other websites
  - Network requests

## Third-Party Services

YouTube Videos Tab does **not** use any third-party services, including:
- No analytics providers (Google Analytics, etc.)
- No crash reporting services
- No advertising networks
- No content delivery networks
